Knife of the Gaucho

It is also considered a symbol of gaucha cultural identity and is often worn as a fashion accessory.

In addition, the gaucho's knife is often associated with honor and courage. In gauchesca culture, it is considered a sign of virility and strength, and gauchos can even swear on their knife as a sign of oath or promise.
